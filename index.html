Our Story

Homemade at Every Step

At Massimo's Beachside Pizza, we believe great pizza starts with real ingredients. We make our dough from scratch, cook our own sauce, and use 100% all-natural mozzarella — because shortcuts have no place in our kitchen.

Located at Pelican Plaza in Vero Beach, FL, we've been a community gathering spot for families, friends, and pizza lovers. Dine in, take out, or get it delivered — every order is made with the same care.
